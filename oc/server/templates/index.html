If you enjoy conversation, you belong here.

OuterClub is an online lounge that acts as your second social life. You and your friends are invited to connect to our global community and discuss your favorite interests and explore new interests. Come participate in a real-time community through instant feeds so you can see and overhear conversations right as they happen, making you a part of a community that is truly alive.


Our mission is to provide users with a platform that's easy to engage in casual conversations with the best real-time experience, and within a lively and social atmosphere.

We love innovation.

An overview of some of our features:

  • Topic templates to simplify discussion creation
  • IRC chat-like user actions
  • Personal blurb
  • Recommended/suggested tasks for exploration
  • Real-time activity notifications
  • Game-like incentives such as leaderboard, up voting, and gift sharing

Passion makes the world go round.

OuterClub runs on passion – a burning desire for a product that we, the founders would love to use. This is about the creation of something new that we can share with our users together. We all have thought-provoking questions about life, interests and hobbies that we love, experiences and stories that we want to share. Let’s cut out all the trolling and flame wars, and broaden our minds and horizons. It’s time to meet the rest of the world and share our passions with it.

It's about both you and me.

We care about our community. At OuterClub, you always have an opportunity to post your opinions and be heard. Your voice matters. Innovating new ideas for a better community experience requires the both of us to work together. Let’s change the world together one step at a time.

  • What is prestige?
    Prestige is a metric used to indicate your likability, popularity, and general quality of contributions. Prestige points are earned when you create posts or when your posts are up voted. When enough prestige points are earned, you increase to the next prestige level.
  • How many prestige levels are there?
    Prestige levels are from 1 to 100. With every prestige level increase, the requirements for prestige points increases.
  • What are the benefits to having high prestige?
    Higher exposure and popularity by being on the leader board, as well as wielding more power. Users with higher prestige have greater influence through up votes.
  • Will there be new categories?
    Yes, depending on demand for new categories.
  • What is Question of the Week?
    Every week an open-ended conversation topic will be chosen. These questions are meant to stimulate community and social interaction.
  • What determines the Suggested Tasks?
    Suggested tasks are our recommendations to you based upon your viewing and posting history. Sometimes you may get recommendations from categories you frequently visit. Other times you might be encouraged to visit conversations you don’t explore often.
  • When should users give a "coffee" up vote?
    The coffee up vote is our representation of a "thumbs up". Our take on up voting is based on people, and not on the actual content itself. Each user can be upvoted once within each conversation. You determine which users deserve your upvote, whether they are: helpful, friendly, spontaneous, badass, or whatever else! In the future, our up vote system will be more elaborate.
  • How does flagging work?
    You can opt to flag a post that you deem is inappropriate, spam, offensive, or bad manner in some way. When enough flags have been voted by the community, the post will shut down automatically. With every flag, the post loses transparency.
  • Who are the moderators?
    Currently the only moderators are the co-founders. As the site expands, we will gradually add qualified moderators. Maintaining a safe and mature community means everything to us.
  • Will users be able to change their avatars?
    Certainly. At the moment, only a limited selection is available. However, we will allow you to upload your own avatar in the near future.
  • Who creates the visuals for OuterClub?
    They’re great, aren’t they? Our lovely graphic designer, Uliya is in charge of all the graphics. The background is photography by Rob Sheridan.
  • When will OuterClub complete beta?
    We are busy making new additions and adjustments on a daily basis. The best answer is: when we feel that the product is finally ready with enough of our intended features. Oh, and of course when it’s bug-free.
  • What new features can we expect in the future?
    We have tons of stuff planned for you guys! This includes: more user customization, better real-time features, conversation facilitation mechanisms, interesting prestige/virtual goods incentives, and so much more.
  • What are personal blurbs?
    A blurb is somewhere around one to two sentences in length describing how you feel about a particular category. It's purpose is to both allow you to express yourself creatively, as well as let others know how you feel about the category.
  • How do I add my friends?
    Those who are current users of OuterClub will be able to invite other users to the community. This will be implemented very soon.
  • How do groups work?
    At the moment, we are in the process of creating groups for your social circles. Groups will provide your mini-community a personal conversation page along with other groups features. However, no prestige can be earned for conversations within the group.

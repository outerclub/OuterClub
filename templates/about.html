About Us

If you enjoy meaningful, you belong here.

TheOuterClub is a social discussion lounge that combines organized discussion with social networking features. Our platform is categorized by a variety of interests and life topics, so that you can view and post discussions that are meaningful to you. It's tough to be part of multiple online communities, so why choose us? The answer is simple - because we are obsessed with facilitating only the highest-quality community discussion.

We innovate discussion.

An overview of some of our unique features:

  • Topic templates (basic, review, poll, storyboard) to simplify thread creation
  • Thread landmarks to provide thread summarization
  • Tags to organize threads and create infinite sub-boards
  • Newsfeed and user following
  • User prestige per board as a form of reputation and incentive for good behavior

Passion makes the world go round.

TheOuterClub runs on passion - a burning desire for a product that we, the founders would love to use. It's not about money. It's not about fame. It's about the creation of something new that we can share with our users together. We all have thought-provoking questions about life, interests and hobbies that we love, experiences and stories that we want to share. Let's cut out all the trolling, flame wars, meaningless and one sentence remarks. Broaden our minds and horizons, it's time to meet the rest of the world and share our passions with it.

It's about both you and me.

We care about our community. At TheOuterClub, you always have an opportunity to post your opinions and be heard. Innovating engaging, high-quality discussion requires the both of us to work together and problem solve for new solutions. Let's change the world together one step at a time.

So let's build a friend ship.
